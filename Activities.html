Volunteer Link

Please wear closed-toed shoes, arrive on time, and cancel your sign-up at least 48 hours in advance if you are unable to keep your volunteer slot. No short shorts, crop shirts, or inappropriate logos on clothing are permitted.

All volunteer hours will be approved at the end of each volunteer opportunity. If your hours are not submitted online, please bring the appropriate paperwork on the day you are volunteering.

Monetary donations are not accepted in exchange for volunteer hours.

We have Monday and Tuesday afternoon weekday opportunities for students. We also have ADULT TRAFFIC opportunities and some set up and break down volunteer slots as we get busy into the holiday season. Our weekend volunteer opportunities are on SATURDAY MORNINGS from 8:00 am - 11:30 am. We hope you can join us on Saturday mornings as we serve the food-insecure community with dignity.

As the holidays are arriving, our line typically grows and we need your help. We are looking for organizations willing to do food drives and also decorate our grocery bags. Please reach out to CatalystChange@gmail.com

We welcome anyone who has time during the week to help us Monday - Thursday between the hours of 9:30 am and 1:30 pm (it does not have to be a full shift - see below) as we navigate sorting donations, stocking shelves, and making grocery bags for our clients. Bring a friend before going out to lunch, come after your morning coffee or workout, or choose a day of the week that you can commit to possibly once per week or month. Our Monday and Thursday morning volunteer spots from 9:30 am - 12:30 pm require our most need.
